Who we are

At Tech Solutions Inc., we are a collective of seasoned professionals with a rich tapestry of experience exceeding 150 years combined. Our expertise spans across seven core IT disciplines: hardware, software, cybersecurity, business continuity, systems integration, cloud services, and networking.

We pride ourselves on managing over 2,500 workstations and servers for more than 100 clients across diverse industries, including legal, real estate, construction, healthcare, accounting services, and retail.

Our Journey

Founded in 1999 in Wilmington, Delaware, Tech Solutions Inc. has been at the forefront of delivering skilled technology services tailored to small and mid-sized businesses. Whether it’s deploying new networks or ensuring the seamless operation of a small office’s PCs, our solutions are designed to address complex IT challenges with simplicity and precision.
